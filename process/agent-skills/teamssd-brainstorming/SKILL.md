# Brainstorming

Inspect local project context, then ask one focused question at a time. Compare
two or three practical approaches with a recommendation. Present a concise
design covering boundaries, data flow, errors and verification, and obtain
human approval before implementation.

Record the approved design under `docs/superpowers/specs/` when durable design
evidence is required, then use `teamssd-writing-plans`.

This variant is text-only. Do not open a browser or load external assets.
